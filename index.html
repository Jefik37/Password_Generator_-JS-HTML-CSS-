PASSWORD GENERATOR

🔐 Option 1

Choose the parameters before creating the password. You can set these parameters however you like, then a password following your specifications will be generated and shown to you.


You can decide which types of characters to include inside your password. Mark the ones you want to see on the password.

🔐 Option 2

You can enter a text between 8 and 50 characters, then a password will be generated from it and shown to you.
